# Consensus Mechanisms

The stability and accuracy of the `qognet` Mesh are maintained through a robust set of cryptoeconomic incentives, ensuring that all three node tiers (Sentinel, Cognito, Validator) are motivated to contribute accurate data and process signals honestly.

The system is designed to reward value creation, which is the accurate and timely discovery of threats, and also aggressively penalizing false, malicious or inaccurate behavior.
